Dante is the iconic protagonist of Devil May Cry (DMC), a popular action-adventure video game series by Capcom. He is a half-demon, half-human hybrid, and the son of the legendary demon Sparda and the human woman Eva. Dante is a demon hunter who runs his own business, Devil May Cry, specializing in eliminating supernatural threats.

Known for his cocky, sarcastic personality and flamboyant combat style, Dante wields a variety of weapons, including his signature sword Rebellion and dual pistols Ebony and Ivory. Throughout the series, he battles numerous demonic foes, including his twin brother Vergil, who often serves as his rival.

Dante's character has evolved over the series, showcasing his humor, strength, and resilience while exploring themes of family, redemption, and his dual nature. His stylish gameplay and charismatic persona have made him one of gaming's most beloved characters.
